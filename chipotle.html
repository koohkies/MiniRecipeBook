Chipotle Bowls

Ingredients

  • bell peppers
  • white rice
  • chicken breast
  • lettuce
  • salsa verde
  • sour cream
  • queso blanco
  • seasonings: salt, garlic powder, smoked paprika, and lemon pepper

Cooking Directions

  1. Wash chicken, then cut them into chunks.
  2. Season the chicken and put into saucepan.
  3. Cook chicken until browned, then put to the side.
  4. Cut bell peppers and put them into saucepan, cook until grilled.
  5. Mix into bowl: bell peppers, chicken breast, white rice, and top with salsa verde, sour cream, and queso blanco!
